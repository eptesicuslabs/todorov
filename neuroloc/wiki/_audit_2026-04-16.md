# wiki audit — 2026-04-16

status: historical context only. frozen as of 2026-04-16. do not edit.

this is the phase 1 deliverable of the 2026-04-16 wiki refactor, governed by
`OPERATING_DIRECTIVE.md`. it catalogs every file under `neuroloc/wiki/`
(206 markdown articles across 9 directories) with a proposed lifecycle
state, freshness signals, and identified work items. the audit itself is
a frozen evidence record; the work items it enumerates are executed in
phases 2-5 of the refactor. corrections to this audit are appended as
`## correction (YYYY-MM-DD)` sections, not edits.

## audit scope and method

four parallel Explore agents surveyed the four main content groupings
(mechanisms/, knowledge+entities/, bridge+comparisons/, synthesis+tests+
mistakes/). the concepts/ directory was surveyed by the driver agent
directly. every file was classified into one of the four lifecycle states
the directive defines: `current`, `superseded`, `historical context only`,
or `definitional`.
