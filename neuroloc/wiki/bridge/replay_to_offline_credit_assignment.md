# bridge: replay to offline credit assignment

status: current (as of 2026-04-23).

## translation

replay is the strongest biological argument that useful learning and refinement need not happen only on the original forward stream. the most relevant project abstraction is:

- replay or reinstatement produces a delayed training surface
- the delayed surface can consolidate, compose, or refine what was learned online

that is closer to offline credit assignment and consolidation than to a passive memory read.

## implications

- replay should be treated as a design axis in future memory theories
- evaluation should include reuse or replay benefit, not only raw recall
- offline or delayed refinement belongs in the backlog theory even while paid compute is paused
