# visual sources systems neuroscience

status: current (as of 2026-04-23).

visual-source registry for systems-neuroscience figures that should be redrawn locally for the wiki and curriculum.

## figure specs

### n1_microcircuit_connectivity_map

- claim: layered cortical microcircuits are structured by laminar and cell-type-specific connectivity, not by one undifferentiated recurrent pool
- source anchor: potjans and diesmann 2014 plus the pd14 reference documentation
- recreation recipe: rebuild the connectivity heatmap from the published connection matrix and redraw a simplified laminar flow diagram
- rights note: redraw only
- target pages: [[canonical_visual_narratives_neuroscience]], [[cross_scale_building_blocks_for_biological_computation]]

### n2_predictive_coding_stack

- claim: predictive coding is best explained as a layered prediction and error-routing architecture rather than as a slogan
- source anchor: rao and ballard 1999; friston 2010 for generalized framing
- recreation recipe: redraw the layered stack and use a toy two-level example rather than paper pixels
- rights note: redraw only
- target pages: [[attention_as_precision_and_routing]], [[canonical_visual_narratives_neuroscience]]

### n4_dendritic_coincidence_and_bac

- claim: bottom-up and top-down coincidence can trigger branch-local plateau events and altered output
- source anchor: shai et al. 2015 and related bac-firing summaries
- recreation recipe: redraw a two-compartment coincidence diagram with a delay window and plateau event
- rights note: redraw only
- target pages: [[cellular_molecular_computational_primitives]], [[canonical_visual_narratives_neuroscience]]
